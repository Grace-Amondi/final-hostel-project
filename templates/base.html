HOW TO..

In the first filter box choose the area around JKUAT that you want to view from using the drop down. Next choose the type of hostel you are looking for in the second filter box.ie bedsitter,single,or one-bedroom. Then click go and the map will zoom to the area of interest.

Click on the marker of one of the hostel and a beautiful pop up showing the owner, price and other details will appear. Click on the book now button to book.

Contacts of the owner will be provided on the pop-ups of each hostel.
